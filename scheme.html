Existing Women Empowerment and Entrepreneurial Schemes

Explore various government schemes that support women entrepreneurs and skill development.

Amma Two-Wheeler Scheme

Provides subsidized two-wheelers to working women to improve mobility and access to workplaces, education centers, and other important locations.

Info Apply
Mahalir Thittam (Women's Development Program)

Aims to improve economic independence through self-help groups (SHGs) for women in rural and urban areas. It provides support for skills training and micro-credit.

Info Apply
Cradle Baby Scheme

Allows mothers in rural areas to safely surrender unwanted girl children without legal repercussions, promoting alternatives to abandonment or harm.

Info Apply
Pudhu Vaazhvu Project

A community-driven initiative focused on poverty reduction, it helps form SHGs, offers training, and promotes entrepreneurship among rural women. Women receive support to engage in productive work and develop small businesses.

Info Apply
Sumangali Scheme

Financial support provided for women’s marriage expenses, especially for economically weaker families. This scheme helps alleviate the financial burden on families and supports the welfare of young women in remote areas.

Info Apply
Tamil Nadu Women Development Corporation (TNWDC)

TNWDC oversees multiple women-centric initiatives, including vocational training programs, education support, and counseling, specifically targeting rural women’s empowerment and economic self-sufficiency.

Info Apply
Matrubhumi Scheme

Aimed at providing health and maternity benefits to women, this scheme is available to mothers in rural areas and covers free medical checkups, nutrition programs, and financial assistance during maternity.

Info Apply
Ujjwala Scheme

Provides free LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) families in rural areas, enabling safe and hygienic cooking practices and reducing health risks associated with traditional cooking methods.

Info Apply
Tamil Nadu Startup and Innovation Mission (TANSIM)

TANSIM offers mentorship, funding, and business support for startups, with specific incentives for women entrepreneurs. It provides incubation support and connects women with venture capital funding, enabling them to establish and scale their businesses in rural and urban areas.

Info Apply
Tamil Nadu Industrial Investment Corporation (TIIC) Women Entrepreneur Loan Scheme

TIIC offers low-interest loans to women entrepreneurs starting businesses in manufacturing or service sectors. It provides loans of up to Rs. 10 lakhs without collateral and prioritizes women-led enterprises, especially in rural and semi-urban regions.
